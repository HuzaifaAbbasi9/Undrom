Home For Landlords

You have a place. We have a tenant.

Exclusive… What’s that?

Undorm fundamentally believes in competition. Since we know that we can get a qualified tenant signed onto a lease before any other brokerage, all we need is the right to compete. For us, exclusives are a nicety, not a necessity.

Around 90% Reduction in Vacancy & Collection Costs

Our pre-leasing model facilitates rapid turnover. We fill your apartment with the next tenant up to 12 months in advance, saving partner landlords more than half a month’s rent worth of downtime. With apartments renting faster, you can practically watch your NOI and property value rise in real time.

Strengthen your rent roll with Flex Leases

3-4 month leases don’t have to be your enemy. Our clients pay above market rents for flexible leasing options and we can have the next tenant lined up for your unit before the lease even starts.

State of the Art Technology, Courtesy of Undorm

Undorm spends more per unit than any other brokerage, dishing out cash to produce state-of-the-art 3D Matterport tours for every unit we have the privilege to list. We want to build a long term relationship with our landlords, and that means investing in you and your property.

Next-Generation Lead-Generation

Any regular brokerage generates leads through two main avenues: personal networks & ad spends. These are inefficient and expensive, respectively. Undorm is different. Student renters come to us to find their apartment because of our brand reputation in universities. We combine this with savvy social media marketing for maximum effect. When students need an apartment, they come to us, first.

Premium Rents, Premium Renters

Undorm provides renters a network greater than just the unit they are in. The connectivity to the Undorm platform bridges the gap between apartments in the free market to the college community. Students pay a massive premium to live in dorms not because they need housing, but they need interconnectedness. Undorm brings that premium to your units by recreating that community through our platform.
